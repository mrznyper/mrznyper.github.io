Overview

 After a period of historic growth in the 80's, Japan's economy collapsed and never recovered. I spotlight the their economic stagnation in the years following Japan's fall from grace, 1990 to present day. Though poor economic policy is mostly to blame, Japan also has a rapidly aging population which is further delaying any hope for recovery.

 In my individual project, I will compare Japan's stock exchange with its international peers to show the rapid growth and subsequent collapse. To highlight their demographic woes, I used census data from Japan and other major countries. My visualization is done primarily with Bokeh to graph and show the economic and demographic trends. Moving average is used to remove some noise in the data from the different exchanges.
